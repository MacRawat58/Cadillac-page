SAFETY

FRONT PEDESTRIAN BRAKING

Front Pedestrian Braking can help you avoid or reduce the severity of a front-end collision with a pedestrian it detects directly ahead of you. It provides pedestrian alerts and can even automatically provide hard emergency braking or enhance the driver’s hard braking. The system works at speeds less than 50 MPH during the daytime. It has limited nighttime and low-visibility performance.

FORWARD COLLISION ALERT

Forward Collision Alert can warn you if it detects a potential front-end collision with a vehicle you’re following, so you can quickly take action. It can also provide a tailgating alert if you’re following a vehicle much too closely.

LANE KEEP ASSIST WITH LANE DEPARTURE WARNING

Lane Keep Assist with Lane Departure Warning uses a brief, gentle steering-wheel turn to alert you when you may be unintentionally drifting out of detected lane lines, so you can steer to stay safely in your lane. If needed, you may receive additional Lane Departure Warning alerts. System alerts do not occur if you’re using your turn signal or if the system detects you may be intentionally leaving your lane.

AUTOMATIC PARKING ASSIST WITH BRAKING

Available Automatic Parking Assist with Braking can search for a parallel or perpendicular parking space, then steer and brake your vehicle into that space using limited braking. While this driver assistance feature steers and tells you when to shift gears, you may need to override it and take control.

NIGHT VISION

Available Night Vision can help you see beyond your headlamps by displaying a thermal heat image that highlights and alerts you to detected pedestrians or large animals directly ahead of you.

LANE CHANGE ALERT WITH SIDE BLIND ZONE ALERT

Available Lane Change Alert with Side Blind Zone Alert can provide side-mirror visual alerts when a detected moving vehicle is quickly approaching or is in your side blind zone. It can help you avoid lane-change collisions.
